FeedIt

Feed It is an exciting endless runner taking place in different locations around the world. A racoon and his fellow companion an ostrich have encountered a hungry beast chasing them trying to eat them. The only way to get the beast out of their back is to feed it mushroom infused dishes. As the partners must cooperate to collect ingredients and cook dishes to keep the beasts off their back they also try to achieve a high score as possible! The duo has to collaborate to try and balance the distance between them and the hungry beasts as they try to get as high of a highscore as possible.

The location has tons of different weird and unique ingredients that combine into all kinds of crazy dishes. Can you figure out the different recipes and build a strategy to beat your friends in this high pace endless runner with tons of mysteries and hidden easter eggs.

Last but not least can you manage to fight off all of the hungry beasts chasing you and can you beat your friends highscore? Partner up with a friend and take on the challenge as the partnership of the racoon and the ostrich! But hurry up, the beasts are hungry!
