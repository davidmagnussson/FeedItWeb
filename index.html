FeedIt

Feed It is an exciting endless runner taking place in different locations around the world. A raccoon and his fellow companion, an ostrich, are hunted by angry beasts trying to eat them. The only way to survive is to feed the beast mushroom infused dishes to make it fall asleep. As the partners must cooperate to collect ingredients and cook dishes to keep the beasts off their back they also try to cook special dishes that yield more points. The duo has to collaborate to try and balance the distance between them and the hungry beasts as they try to get as high of a score as possible.

The locations has tons of different weird and unique ingredients that combine into all kinds of crazy dishes. Can you figure out all the different recipes and build a strategy to beat your friends in this high pace endless runner with tons of mysteries and hidden Easter eggs?

Partner up with a friend and take on the challenge as the partnership of the raccoon and the ostrich! But hurry up, the beasts are hungry!
